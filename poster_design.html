Product Description

Ayam Kings Ordering System is a comprehensive web-based food ordering solution designed to digitize restaurant operations. The system bridges the gap between customers, kitchen staff, and administrators through a unified platform featuring seamless ordering, real-time status tracking, and automated financial reporting.

Key Features

  • ToyyibPay Payment Gateway: Secure online payment integration supporting FPX, Cards, and QR payments.
  • Google OAuth Login: One-click registration and login for a frictionless customer experience.
  • Live Order Tracking: Real-time status updates (Pending, Preparing, Ready, Completed) with visual status badges.
  • Smart Coupon System: Validation logic for discount codes to manage marketing campaigns effectively.
  • Staff & Admin Dashboards: Dedicated portals for order processing and full menu/user management.

Novelty & Inventiveness

  • Integrated Financial Analytics: Automated generation of financial reports with visual charts and downloadable CSV/PDF support.
  • Dynamic Daily Specials: A dedicated carousel module for time-sensitive promotions and daily specials to drive impulse purchases.
  • Review & Rating Ecosystem: Built-in customer feedback loop allowing star ratings and comments, restricted to verified purchasers to ensure authenticity.
